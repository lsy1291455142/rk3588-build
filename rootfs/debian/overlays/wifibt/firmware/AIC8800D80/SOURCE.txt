Preferred source: Radxa aic8800-firmware deb (not a Debian archive package).

  https://github.com/radxa-pkg/aic8800/releases
  package: aic8800-firmware_*.deb  (Architecture: all)

Stage into this overlay:

  ./rootfs/debian/overlays/wifibt/sync-assets.sh --deb-aic
  # or
  ./rootfs/debian/overlays/wifibt/sync-assets.sh --deb /path/to/aic8800-firmware_*.deb

Build then uses WIFIBT_CHIP=AIC8800D80 (board default on CokePi):
  package is extracted and remapped to /lib/firmware/aic8800D80/
  with Rockchip /vendor -> /system -> /lib/firmware links.

SOURCE.txt alone does not count as firmware.
