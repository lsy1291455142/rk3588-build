AIC8800D80 firmware for CokePi Model LP4 V1.0 (RK3588S).

Stage from Radxa aic8800-firmware deb (host-side, not during build):

  ./rootfs/debian/boards/rk3588s-cokepi-model-lp4-v10/stage-aic8800-firmware.sh

Or:
  ./rootfs/debian/boards/rk3588s-cokepi-model-lp4-v10/stage-aic8800-firmware.sh \
    /path/to/aic8800-firmware_*.deb

This populates lib/firmware/aic8800D80/ from the deb's
  lib/firmware/aic8800_fw/SDIO/aic8800D80/

SOURCE.txt alone is not firmware; WiFi needs the staged blobs.
